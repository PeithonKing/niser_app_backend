What is this?
NISER Archive is a community-run project to archive all the courses offered at NISER.
What kind of content are we archiving?
Everything, related to the course: quizzes, handouts, notes, slides, mid/end-term exams, etc.
Who uploads the content?
You!
This is a community-run project, all the material, relevant to a course, is uploaded to the website by users (such as yourself).
How to contribute?
You can help the project in 3 ways:
  • By uploading content: The primary way to contribute to the project, is to upload anything you have, relevant to the courses, that you have taken at NISER. At the very least, for the courses, that you are currently enrolled into, you can upload the quizzes and other exams on the day, they are held. And, if you are feeling generous, you can dig through your heap of papers, that you've piled up during your time at NISER, and upload them too. Note that, we don't expect the materials to be complete. Just anything, which gives a hint of what was done during the course, is good enough.
  • By becoming a moderator: Ever envied those people on reddit, who have the power to delete posts at their will? Or, those people on StackExchange, with hats, who can close a question, just because, they deemed it to be off-topic? Well, you can gain similar powers by becoming a moderator on Archive, and keeping the discussions civil.
  • By contributing code: If you are familiar with any of these: Python, django, Apache, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript or bash then you may help the project by contributing code to the github repo for the Archive.
How to contact the developers?
We can be reached at sdg@niser.ac.in or code@niser.ac.in. Please feel free to drop in any suggestions, issues, improvements or queries. We will get back to you as soon as we can.
